New Patient Intake
All new patients complete a secure digital health history form through Jane App before their first appointment. You'll receive a link by email after booking. Please complete forms at least 24 hours before your visit.

Payments & Cancellations
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, HSA/FSA cards, and cash. A 24-hour cancellation notice is required to avoid a cancellation fee. Same-day cancellations may be subject to a $50 fee.
